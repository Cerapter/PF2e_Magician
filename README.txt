Bring the wonder of performance magic to your Pathfinder games! The magician is a character class that allows you to manipulate your enemies with subtle tricks to leave them open to your strikes. A martial class with occult magic-like effects that is fully remaster compatible.

Full PDF available freely here: https://www.dropbox.com/scl/fi/x99lklv31t4t5ozgk6pam/Magician.pdf?rlkey=qqqz299nz4qb1l5enidyom4no&e=1&dl=0

This repo contains implementations of the class in both Foundry VTT and Pathbuilder.
